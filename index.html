On writing —

I love fiction and I've been an avid reader since I was a little girl, so it was only time for me to start writing

I primarily write speculative fiction, exploring themes of identity, technology, and the human condition.

My work is either a solarpunk hopeful story or the worse tragedy in space opera ever written! Have a look on the stories tab to see more.

I ocasionally write non fiction on my substack, and am trying to get it published to other places too!

On AI usage —

I do not use AI in my creative practice, at all. I don't research with AI, i don't plot with AI, i don't write with AI and i don't edit with AI.

I cannot say that i do not use AI at all because as a software engineer, it is a requirement of my job, but i try to use is the least possible so that i make my manager happy but also keep my braincells hydrated!
